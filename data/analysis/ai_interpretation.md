AI systems may interpret water quality data in a simplified way by treating all variables as independent numerical inputs.

Potential limitations include:
- Ignoring environmental context (season, temperature, ecosystem behavior)
- Overlooking biological relationships (e.g., algae growth vs turbidity)
- Misclassifying normal operational variation as anomalies
- Treating RPD values without understanding laboratory QA/QC standards

This may lead to incomplete or misleading interpretations if not guided by domain expertise.
